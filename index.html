    Administration & Tools

    This survey will be conducted online at Lifebridgehealth.org using an invitation popup. To determine the available functionality of the survey, we will have to select a tool, and have provided a brief summary of the available options below.

    Overall, we recommend the free tier of Hotjar for its ability to ask unlimited questions and receive unlimited responses, its moderate range of question types, and embed display options.

    With the Hotjar code added to the site, we can also collect continuous heatmapping data from the homepage (and/or any other key pages on the site) to determine where users are most drawn to, and what parts of the page they click the most.
