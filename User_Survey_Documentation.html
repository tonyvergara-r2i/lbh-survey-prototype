SURVEY BACKGROUND

The website redesign feedback survey for LifeBridge Health was developed to collect information on key issues and opportunities that we should know about as we begin to redesign the website. We aim to improve the content of this survey through an iterative review process.

Survey Goals

  1. Determine Information Needs

    What information people are looking for as they seek care at LBH?

  2. Benchmark Website Experience

    How do people feel about their experience on the LBH website?

  3. Understand Audience Segmentation

    Who are the user groups coming to the website? How prevalent are these groups?

Population Targeting & Sampling

We will present this survey to all users who land on the website, in order to collect the widest range of data from all LifeBridge Health audiences. We've opted to use the bottom of page popup display type to ensure that the feedback survey does not cover the screen and impede the user's ability to find their actual content on the website to minimize the survey's impact to their experience on the website.

Administration & Tools

This survey will be conducted online at Lifebridgehealth.org website using an invitation popup, and have provided a brief summary of the available options below.

Overall, we recommend the free tier of Hotjar for its ability to ask unlimited questions and receive unlimited responses, its moderate range of question types, and embed display options.

With the Hotjar code added to the site, we can also collect continuous heatmapping data from the homepage (and/or any other key pages on the site) to determine where users are most drawn to and what parts of the page they click the most. Ensuring Quality

To ensure quality data, we recommend collecting as many feedback survey responses as possible, to account for diversity in the user groups and ideally allow for segmentation of the data (depending on the number of responses received).

Survey Questions

Introduction Lanuguage

We're collecting information to prepare for a website redesign and are interested in your feedback.

If you're interested in participating, click "Next" below. This survey should take approximately 5 minutes of your time.

Thanks!


Alternative Intro

Got 5 min? Answer a few quick questions to help us improve the LifeBridge Health website!


Experience Questions

To better understand the user's experience on the website, we intend to ask the following questions.

  • What brings you to the website today?
  • What is your overall impression of the website?
  • How would you rate the design of the website?
  • How useful was the information found on the website?
  • How easy to use was the website?
  • What is the best thing about the website?
  • What about the website could use improvement?
  • How easy was it to find what you were looking for on the site?
  • Is there any other feedback you would like to share?
